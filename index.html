About me

My name is Amir M. Ahmadian and I am a PhD student at the Theoretical Computer Science (TCS) Division of KTH Royal Institute of Technology, working under the supervision of Musard Balliu and Dilian Gurov. Prior to KTH, I was a M.Sc. student at Amirkabir University of Technology working on Secret Sharing schemes and Steganography.

Research Interests

My PhD research is currently focused on the intersection of security policies, software security, and programming languages.

More broadly, my research interests are:

  • Language-based security
  • Information flow control
  • Formal methods
  • Software security
